2025 National Burned Area Emergency Response Readiness Meeting

Forest Service Water Erosion Prediction Project, FSWEPP Suite of Tools

3-4 June 2025, Tuesday and Wednesday

Radisson Hotel, 215 West South Temple, Salt Lake City, Utah (385-354-5457)

Updated: 16 May 2025

Meeting Objectives

Provide participants with knowledge and skills to perform advanced analysis using the Forest Service Suite of Water Erosion Prediction Project, FSWEPP Tools for wildfire emergencies.
